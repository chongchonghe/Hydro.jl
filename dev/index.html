Hydro.hll1st — Method

HLL Riemann solver, 1st order. Piecewise constant construction is applied where the left and right states on the interface are simply U{i+1/2},L = u{i} U{i+1/2},R = u{i+1} The wave-speeds are estimated via the simple expression: SL = min{UL - aL, UR - aR} SR = max{UL + aL, UR + aR}
